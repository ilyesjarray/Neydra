[ HOW TO USE - GLOBAL NEYDRA NEWS ]

AUTOMATIC MODE (Default)

The news feed automatically fetches news from multiple free sources worldwide. No setup required!

  • 1
    Browse News

    Articles load automatically from GDELT, BBC, Reuters, Al Jazeera, and more.

  • 2
    Interact with the Globe

    Click and drag the 3D earth. Click news articles to fly to their location.

  • 3
    Watch Live TV

    Switch between Al Jazeera, France 24, and DW News live streams in the left panel.

  • 4
    Filter News

    Use category buttons (WORLD, TECH, BIZ, etc.) and language switcher (EN, FR, AR, ES, DE).

  • 5
    Urgent Alerts

    Breaking news triggers the red URGENT bar at the bottom with an alert sound.

KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS

  • R
    Refresh

    Force refresh all news sources.

  • F
    Fullscreen

    Toggle fullscreen mode for the earth view.

  • M
    Mute

    Toggle alert sounds on/off.

  • W
    Wireframe

    Toggle between textured and wireframe earth modes.

LEGACY BACKEND MODE (Optional)

  • !
    Use Legacy Backend

    Click "LEGACY MODE" in controls to connect to the old backend server via LocalTunnel.
